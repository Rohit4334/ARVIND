# 🛡️ ARVIND

# Fraud Intelligence Platform

### Understand • Investigate • Decide

ARVIND is an explainable fraud intelligence platform designed to demonstrate how modern payment fraud investigations can move beyond simple risk scoring into evidence-driven decision making.

Unlike traditional fraud detection projects that focus solely on machine learning predictions, ARVIND combines fraud rules, evidence generation, case management and investigator workflows into a modular platform inspired by enterprise fraud operations.

---

# Why I Built ARVIND

Having worked extensively in Payments, Fraud Operations and Financial Crime, I wanted to build a platform that reflects how fraud analysts actually investigate suspicious transactions.

Most demonstration projects stop at predicting fraud.

Real fraud teams need to:

- Understand why a transaction is risky.
- Review supporting evidence.
- Investigate cases.
- Collaborate with analysts.
- Record investigation notes.
- Make explainable decisions.

ARVIND has been designed around that workflow.
